About Us

SolarSys Solutions was founded in 2010 with a mission to make renewable energy accessible to everyone. Our team of certified engineers and technicians have decades of experience in the energy sector.

We pride ourselves on delivering high-quality, sustainable solutions that not only meet our clients' needs but also contribute to a greener planet. Our comprehensive approach ensures that every project is completed to the highest standards of excellence.
